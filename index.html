Aims of the network

Our aims our to improve the evidence base for infection in pregnancy and post-partum

Connect expertise

  • Bring together clinicians, researchers and collaborators working across maternal infection.
  • Create opportunities for collaboration across overlapping interests and methods.

Share knowledge

  • Provide a route to useful resources, outputs and practical information.
  • Support discussion of questions, priorities and emerging evidence gaps.

Support projects

  • Highlight active work across the network and make it easier for others to get involved.
  • Build visibility for early and developing collaborative work.

Working groups

Below are the list of the different working groups.

Group 1

Maternal, post-partum, breastfeeding and fetal PK-PD

Pharmacokinetics and pharmacodynamics across pregnancy-related settings.

 Group 2

Practice variation e.g. intrapartum delivery

Variation in pathways, decisions and implementation across care contexts.

 Group 3

Asymptomatic bacteriuria

Screening, diagnosis, treatment choices and evidence gaps.

 Group 4

Breastfeeding, lactation and infant safety

Infant exposure, drug safety and practical guidance during lactation.

 Group 5

Antibiotic usage in labour / post-partum

Stewardship, indication patterns and practical antimicrobial use.

 Group 6

Surgical site infections

Prevention, surveillance and management in maternal care pathways.
