Current Chair and Founder

Dr. Sathyavani Subbarao, is a Specialist Registrar in Medical Microbiology and Infectious Diseases and chair of the Maternal Infection Network.

Her clinical and academic interests include antimicrobial pharmacokinetics and pharmacodynamics in pregnancy, antimicrobial resistance, and managing difficult-to-treat infections such as bone and joint infections.
